## 🔐 Cybersecurity Policy Development Project

**(GDPR / HIPAA / NIST / ISO/IEC Aligned)**

### 📌 Project Overview

This project demonstrates the development of a comprehensive set of cybersecurity policies designed to protect **Naztech** information assets, employees, and operational systems from modern cyber threats using template from sans.org

The initiative focuses on aligning organizational policies with global legal requirements such as **GDPR** and **HIPAA**, as well as internationally recognized standards including **NIST** and **ISO/IEC 27001**. The goal was to establish a structured, compliant, and risk-driven policy framework that supports secure business operations.

---

### 🎯 Business Purpose

In today’s threat landscape, organizations face increasing risks from:

* Data breaches
* Ransomware and malware attacks
* Unauthorized access to systems
* Compromise of network services
* Legal and regulatory penalties

These policies were created to reduce these risks, ensure compliance, and protect both the company and its employees from financial, operational, and reputational damage.

### 📏 Standards & Frameworks Applied

* **NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF):** Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond
* **ISO/IEC 27001:** Information security management and control structure
* **CIS Controls:** Best practices for securing systems and data

These frameworks guided the structure, content, and effectiveness of each policy.

---

### 🛠️ Policy Development Process

The policies were created following a structured and repeatable approach:

1. **Risk Identification**

   * Identified key threats (e.g., ransomware, insider threats, unauthorized access)

2. **Requirement Analysis**

   * Reviewed legal obligations (GDPR, HIPAA) and industry standards

3. **Control Mapping**

   * Mapped risks to NIST and ISO/IEC controls

4. **Policy Drafting**

   * Created clear, enforceable, and business-aligned policy documents

5. **Review & Validation**

   * Ensured policies are practical, compliant, and aligned with business needs

6. **Implementation Considerations**

   * Focused on usability, employee awareness, and enforceability

---

### 📄 Policies Developed

The following policies were created as part of this project:

1. **Acceptable Use Standard**
   Defines appropriate use of company systems, devices, and network resources

2. **Privacy Policy**
   Establishes how personal and sensitive data is collected, processed, and protected

3. **Password Policy**
   Enforces strong authentication practices to prevent unauthorized access

4. **Access Management Policy**
   Defines how user access is granted, reviewed, and revoked

5. **Access Controls Standard**
   Implements least privilege and role-based access control principles

6. **Incident Response Policy**
   Provides a structured approach to detecting, responding to, and recovering from security incidents
